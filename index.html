WELCOME TO

KVUPS PANGODE

K V U P S is an aided school established in 1964 at Palode sub-district,
Trivandrum, which faced the threat of shutting down due to the lack of students
in 1980. Now this school is one of the most commonly studied public schools in
Palode sub-district. A concise details about the school’s current activities are
given below.

Our

Mission

Our mission is to play a major role in education
sector by providing a quality education through strict discipline based on moral values to achieve this,the harmonious co-
operation of the parents, teachers and students are inevitablethe satisfied parents,
educationaly enlightened scholars, professionaly skilled teachers and a highly principled
management are the highlightedfeatures of this institution.

Our

Vission

Our vision is to empower students to accure, demonstrate, articulate and value
knowledge and skills that will support them, as a life long learners, to
participate in and contribute to the global world and
practice.The core value of the school is :respect,tolerance, inclution and excellence.

Systematic academic activities


The annual plan which is prepared before the beginning of the academic year by
the PTA and SRG helps in the systematic functioning of the school.

KALLARA-PANGODE MELA


The programmes as a part of the annual celebrations of the school in 2000 was later adopted by the development committee & natives and is now known as Kallara - Pangode fest or as “Magical Fest in the historical world”, is a famous regional cultural fest. From the last fifteen years after the mid summer exams the one week long fest begins with the PTA meetings.

OUR FACILITIES


SMART CLASSROOMS



We provide an international facility to enrich our student's skills related to technology and academy. All the teachers are trained to classes by smartclassroom and pupils are very interested to attend those classes that develop their all over perfomance.


COMPUTER LAB



Our computer lab is well furnished and equip oriented. Specially trained teachers are leading each classes.


LIBRARY



One special period in every week is allotted for library and a concerned teacher leads the students to the library. Students are given details regarding the distribution of books. The library equipped with thousands of books has granted the students lifetime membership. The open library is accessible to the public after school hours and during holidays.
